Dein Last.fm wird NIEMALS gespeichert. It is used once, hashed, to get a permission "key" so Koto can scrobble and update your Now Playing on Last.fm. Since the password is not saved, you can revoke Koto's permission on Last.fm and it will not be able to scrobble unless you log in again.

Dein Twitter Passwort wird niemals gespeichert. It is used once, hashed, to get a permission "key" so Koto can update your statuses when you ask it to. Since the password is not saved, you can revoke Koto's permission on Twitter and it will no longer work (unless you log in again).
